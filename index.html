ZURICH 2022

Kubernetes...!!!

At a Glance

The conference - January 31 Kubernetes has become the de-facto standard for container orchestration and management of distributed applications. Led by specialists of the industry, this one day conference aims to brings users and representatives of the ecosystem and the larger CNCF community together to describe how the entire "Container Stack" can be built and implemented. A mixed balance of user stories from experts in the Pharmaceutical, Banking, Media and Research industries plus representatives of the worldwide Kubernetes and CNCF ecosystem will talk about their experiences and their solutions to the challenges of big cloud native architectures. Experts from the networking layer, storage layer, packaging layer and professional services will distill their know-how and bring their worldwide expertise to the audience.
